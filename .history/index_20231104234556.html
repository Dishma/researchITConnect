About ITConnect Research


ITConnect is a real-time personalized job portal for IT professionals.
Main objective - To implement a real-time personalized job posting website for IT industry.


Literature Review

  • Embark on a journey through the latest developments in online job portals with our comprehensive research review. Explore four key areas of innovation shaping the future of job search platforms.
  • First, delve into Fraudulent Job Advertisement Detection, where advanced machine learning algorithms meticulously sift through job postings, ensuring the authenticity of listings. Discover the intricacies of Efficient Job Recommendation Systems, where deep learning techniques and Natural Language Processing (NLP) create personalized matches, transforming the job hunting experience.
  • Next, explore Resume Evaluation and Enhancement, where the integration of NLP and deep learning technologies provides a detailed analysis of resumes. Advanced models, including bidirectional Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, offer nuanced insights, surpassing conventional methods. Finally, dive into Face Detection, Verification, and Stress Detection, witnessing the synergy of computer vision and machine learning in creating robust security measures.
