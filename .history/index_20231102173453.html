About - ITConnect Research


ITConnect is a real-time personalized job portal for IT professionals Main objective - To implement a real-time personalized job posting website for IT industry.


Main Research Problem.

  • How an effective job portal benefits both job seeks and recruiters ?

Objectives.

  • Accurate classification of fake job posts
  • To recommend personalized jobs based on user’s skill test results and resume data
  • Verify candidates and detect Stress level by non-verbal behaviors
  • CV recommendation and enhancement system
